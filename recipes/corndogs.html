Corn Dog

Ingredients:

  1. Cornmeal and flour: This corn dog batter starts with yellow cornmeal and all-purpose flour.
  2. Sugar: A pinch of sugar lends a hint of sweetness.
  3. Baking powder: Baking powder acts as leavener, which means it helps the batter rise.
  4. Salt and pepper: Salt enhances the flavours of the other Ingredients.
  5. Milk: A cup of milk adds moisture, ensuring a tender coating.
  6. Egg: An egg gives the batter even more moisture and acts as a binder, which means it helps hold the batter together.
  7. Beef frankfurters: This recipe calls for beef frankfurters, but you can use pork franks (or hot dogs) if you'd prefer.

Step:

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry Ingredients, the stirring in the milk and egg.
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter.
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned.
