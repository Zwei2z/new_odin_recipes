French Onion Soup

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  3. 4 cups sliced onions
  4. 5 cups beef broth
  5. 2 tablespoons dry sherry
  6. 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  7. 1 pinch salt and pepper
  8. 4 slices French bread
  9. 4 slices provolone cheese
  10. 2 slices Swiss cheese
  11. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step

  1. Melt butter with olive oil in an 8-quart stock pot over medium heat. Add onions to butter and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  2. Add beef broth, sherry, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven's broiler.
  4. Ladle soup into oven-safe servings bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each. Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese.
  5. Place bowls on a cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and brown slightly, 2 to 3 minutes.
